Welcome!

The Tim Hortons National Museum (THNM) welcomes you. We were given a mission. Tim Hortons chose us. Figuratively. We could not avoid its fertile roussettes au miel sowing our streets. The THNM is a playful space, where the visitors can observe the weird and wonderful merchandise we gathered. Also, our visitors are encouraged to manipulate the objects on display. The THNM is also a space dedicated to research, reasoning, and collective creation on a plurality of themes. Notably on cultural identity, the occupation of unceded land, and workplace conditions. All these issues are discussed through the lens of the famous coffee and donut company.
